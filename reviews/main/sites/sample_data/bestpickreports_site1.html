Company Information
Services Offered
Russell Roofing & Exteriors builds and installs seamless aluminum and custom-fabricated copper gutter systems for residential and commercial customers. The company also replaces and repairs slate, tile, copper, metal, asphalt, and cedar roofs as well as all flat roof systems.
Warranty
The company provides lifetime warranties on labor and materials through GAF and CertainTeed.
Company History
Russell Roofing began in 1992.
Distinctions
The company has won several national, regional, and local awards. Russell Roofing & Exteriors is certified at the highest level with GAF as a Master Elite Roofer and with CertainTeed as a Select ShingleMaster.
Employee Information
Employees undergo drug testing, background checks, and driver's license checks.
