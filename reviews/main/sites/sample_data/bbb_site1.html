      Dana L.

      (5 stars)

      03/10/2021

      We were looking to have some roofing/siding work done. We needed to repair a leaking roof over our garage, and replace the second story, and on two outbuildings. We also had them install new siding on the side of the house where we had the leak and new AZEK trim around our garage doors. The project manger was great to work with and kept us informed during the entire process. The crew was professional and extremely hardworking. They were meticulous and we could not be happier about the finished product. They did a great job matching the roofing and siding to the existing color, and also did a wonderful job with clean up. We ended up needing to replace 2/3 of the plywood on the second story and that process went beautifully and did not hold up the project. My wife had used them in the past and was happy so we went with them after reviewing multiple bids. The price was right in line with others. The office staff was great to work with and kept us informed on the scheduling. In the end the quality of the work and professionalism of the entire Rutter organization was outstanding and is what sets them apart from others. We highly recommend Rutter Roofing.
